How this works
1. Auto-fill: Spare capacity from your schedule appears here automatically.
2. Toggle off any row you don't want to share. It stays in your records but won't be broadcast.
3. Edit a row to update route, capacity, or truck details before broadcasting.
4. Encrypt & Broadcast pushes your HMAC-blinded tokens to the network. Supply doesn't match anything — it just broadcasts. Demand is the only side that runs a match.
